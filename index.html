    About the Principal
    Psychologist & Hypnotherapist

    Samantha Tay

    She is a Principal Clinical Psychologist & Hypnotherapist, experience in administering psychological assessment, integrated psychotherapy & Hypnotherapy. Using different psychotherapeutic modalities such as CBT, EMDR, REBT and Hypnotherapy for the treatment of Depression, PTSD and other psychological disorders . She is also a member of the Association of Psychotherapist and Counsellors (APAC) in Singapore.She has mastered the art of Hypnotherapy and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy which she employs to treat psychological issues effectively.Her techniques have shown promising outcomes for her clients to battle against issues like Panic Attack, Anxiety, Youth & Adolescent issues, Grief and other Trauma & mental health issues.She does not only help people overcome their psychological issues but also helps them unleash their hidden talents and discover their ultimate potential.Her special interests include corporate coaching, crisis management and career counselling.
